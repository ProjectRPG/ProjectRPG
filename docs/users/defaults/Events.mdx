# Built-in Events

Project RPG listens to various Minecraft events to trigger RPG mechanics.

## Level Up

Triggered when player gains a level in progression.

## Anvil Repair

When repairing items on an anvil.

## Break Block

When a block is broken.

## Break Speed

When calculating break speed for blocks.

## Place Block

When a block is placed.

## Breath Change

When air supply changes (every 10 ticks).

## Breed Animal

When animals breed.

## Tame Animal

When an animal is tamed.

## Brew Potion

When brewing potions.

## Consume

When consuming food items.

## Item Crafted

When crafting items.

## On Death

When an entity dies.

## Effect Added

When a mob effect is applied.

## Player Fish

When fishing yields items.

## Heal

When player heals.

## Jump

When player jumps normally.

## Sprint Jump

When jumping while sprinting.

## Crouch Jump

When jumping while crouching.

## Player Attack Entity

When player attacks an entity.

## Damaged By Player

When an entity is damaged by player.

## Entity Damage Player

When player is damaged by an entity.

## Damage Player

General damage events to player.

## Mitigated Damage

Various damage mitigation events (total, armor, absorption, effects, enchants, block).

## Sprinting

When sprinting (every 10 ticks).

## Submerged

When underwater (every 10 ticks).

## Swimming

When swimming (every 10 ticks).

## Diving

When diving underwater.

## Surfacing

When surfacing from underwater.

## Swim Sprinting

When sprinting while swimming.

## Riding

When riding entities (every 10 ticks).

## Finish Use Item

When finishing using an item.

## Use Item

When right-clicking with an item.

## Enchant Item

When enchanting items.
